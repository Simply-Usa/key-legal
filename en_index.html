What accreditation is?

Accreditation of Branches and Representative offices

Quick start of your business in Russia

Your company plans to expand business and to enter Russian market? For doing business of a foreign company in Russia it is necessary to:

  • choose a business form (through a representative office or branch, or by opening of a subsidiary);
  • obtain accreditation of a branch/representative office at the authorised public authority;
  • register at tax authority, open settlement account in a bank and to carry out other formalities.

Accreditation of a Branch

A branch - is a separate division of a legal entity situated outside the location of the legal entity. A branch can be endowed with all the functions of the legal entity that opened it, or you can transfer only part of the functions of the main legal entity to the Branch. The functions of the Branch are specified in the Regulation on the Branch. The Branch is managed by the Director / Head of the Branch on the basis of a power of attorney issued by the General Director of the legal entity. Branches of foreign organizations have the right to carry out their activities on the basis of a special permit issued by a tax authority

Accreditation of Representative offices

A representative office - is a separate division of a legal entity situated outside the location of the legal entity. Representative office is limited in its functions to representing and protecting the interests of the company that opened it. The functions of the representative office are specified in the Regulation on the representative office. The representative office is headed by the director / head of the representative office on the basis of a power of attorney issued by the General director of the legal entity. Representative offices of foreign organizations have the right to carry out their activities on the basis of a special permit issued by a tax authority (accreditation).

Legal recommendations regarding a business form choice

One of the most difficult issues for many foreign companies is the question of how to start a business in Russia - in the form of a subsidiary, a branch of a foreign company or a representative office of a foreign company. First of all, in order to register a representative office in Russia it is necessary to determine the list of its activities. Key Legal lawyers are capable of asking right questions and offering solutions even for unusual/non-standard situations. We will help you to find the best solution and give recommendations to your company about the most convenient and legally correct form of doing business in Russia.

If we open a Branch / Representative Office not in Moscow, can you help us?

of course yes, the accreditation of a Branch / Representative Office for all regions of the Russian Federation is carried out in Moscow.

What form of documents is required for accreditation of a Branch / Representative Office?

All documents issued by a foreign organization must be notarized and apostilled, with a notarized translation into Russian.

Do you always need an apostille on documents coming from a foreign company?

no, in some cases (for example, for companies from China) legalization of the document is required; in cases where there is a Mutual assistance agreement between Russia and a foreign state, an apostille is not needed (for example, for companies from Estonia).

Are notarized copies of documents of the parent company suitable for accreditation?

depends on the type of document, if we are talking about the Charter or Registration Certificate of a foreign company, then notarized, legalized / apostilled copies are suitable.
